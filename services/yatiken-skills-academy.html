Yatiken Skills Academy

Yatiken brings to you work integrated skill-based higher education in association with Medhavi Skills University. With over half of its population under 25 and over 62% of its population in the working age group, India has enormous potential to become a global talent powerhouse. There is an urgent need to rethink higher education as a catalyst for providing the youth with the necessary information, skills, and opportunities for contributing to the country’s growth trajectory in order to effectively utilize this demographic dividend.

Upskilling can bridge the gap between academics and industry by incorporating real-world experiences and industry-aligned curriculum, guaranteeing graduates are not just job-ready but also competent to promote societal change.

Partner with us and upskill your students with short-term skilled programs under the following schemes

PMKVY 4.0

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, or PMKVY, is the government of India's flagship program designed to train young people in a variety of areas in skill development. People can become certified and gain industry-relevant skills through PMKVY, which will improve their employability and help the country's workforce develop.

PM Vishwakarma Scheme

The PM Vishwakarma Scheme is designed to foster skill development and entrepreneurship in the manufacturing industry. To foster innovation and growth in the manufacturing sector, it offers financial support, training programs, and mentorship to small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) and startups.

Work Integrated Program

To prepare students for the workforce, a work-integrated program combines classroom instruction with real-world work experience. By providing participants with practical skills, industry insights, and real-world challenges—all crucial for career readiness—this program closes the knowledge gap between theory and practice.

Learn And Earn Program

This program provides a special chance for people to learn while obtaining real-world employment experience. Through training courses and workshops, participants can develop their skills while earning money through internships or part-time work, encouraging a culture of lifelong learning and financial independence.

Flexi ITI Program

This technical education program offers students freedom and customization by letting them select specialized modules according to their interests and professional aspirations. This program offers a variety of courses and programs to improve technical skills and employability in the sector, catering to a wide range of learning needs.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) refers to the efforts undertaken by corporations to support environmental and social causes in addition to their profit-making goals. Companies show their dedication to social well-being and moral business practices by participating in CSR activities such as community development projects, environmental sustainability efforts, education, and healthcare initiatives.
