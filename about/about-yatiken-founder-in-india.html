Alok Kashyap

Founder & CEO of Yatiken

He is the driving force behind Yatiken, India’s leading software solutions company. He has over a decade of experience as a Project Manager and Scrum Master. Alok has earned a reputation for turning complex ideas into powerful, real-world solutions. Hence, he has collaborated with global teams and industry giants like United Airlines, Alok brings a unique blend of strategic insight and hands-on expertise to every project.

His vision is to create a company where cutting-edge technology meets client-centered solutions. By fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and accountability, Alok has built Yatiken into a trusted partner for businesses looking to scale, streamline, and succeed. His leadership empowers a skilled team to transform business challenges into success stories.

From consulting with stakeholders to guiding each project milestone, Alok’s commitment to excellence ensures Yatiken consistently delivers results that exceed client expectations. As a founder with global experience, he understands the nuances of both domestic and international markets, enabling Yatiken to offer solutions that are not only innovative but also deeply relevant.

Alok believes that a good leader is one who thinks that it is not just about building technology – it’s about building relationships, driving growth, and achieving meaningful impact. His journey from a determined tech professional to the CEO of a rapidly growing company has been a story of resilience, innovation, and relentless pursuit of excellence.
